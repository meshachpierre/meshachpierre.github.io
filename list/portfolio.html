Thank you for viewing my photography portfolio.

I mostly take photos of nature, although I do experiment with other styles, techniques, and subjects. I approach photography with the goal of storytelling. I believe that any camera is capable of taking visually pleasing images. Although I mainly use a Nikon DSLR, I also use my phone camera quite a bit. I generally shoot RAW and post-process most images using a combination of Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom.

If you have any questions or would like to inquire about licensing images, prints, or otherwise, please email me.
